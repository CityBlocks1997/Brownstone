🏙️ How CityBlocks Works

  • 🎭 Physical Graffiti Style: Inspired by Led Zeppelin's iconic album cover
  • 🏠 Host Window (Top): The owner's window featuring the final combined performance
  • 🎬 12 Collaborator Windows: Each window showcases a different creator's contribution
  • 🔒 Public or Private: Choose whether your block is open to all or invite-only
  • 🎨 Custom Backgrounds: Select different building styles for your homepage
  • ✨ Pure Collaboration: No politics - just creators building together
