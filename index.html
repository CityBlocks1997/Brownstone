How CityBlocks Works

  • 🏠 Each Creator Gets a Block: Your personal brownstone to host collaborative projects
  • 🎭 12 Collaborator Windows: Invite singers, musicians, animators, voice actors, and more
  • 🎬 Host Window: The top window showcases the final combined performance
  • 🔒 Private or Public: Keep blocks private during creation, then share when ready
  • ✨ Pure Collaboration: No politics, no algorithms - just creators building together
