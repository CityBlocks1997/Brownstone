How CityBlocks Works

  • 🏠 Each Block is a Building: Your brownstone hosts collaborative projects
  • 🎭 13 Windows: 1 host window (top) + 12 collaborator windows
  • 🎬 Hollywood Squares Style: Each window shows a different creator's performance
  • ✨ Pure Collaboration: No politics - just creators building together
